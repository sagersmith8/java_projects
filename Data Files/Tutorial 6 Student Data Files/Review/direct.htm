Directions and Parking

Caution!

Because of on-going construction at Exit 42 on the Elizabeth Parkway, you might want to take the Sallings River Drive, rather than the Parkway.

Exit at Hastings Blvd. (Exit 43) and follow the signs to navigate through the detours.

Construction is in progress Monday through Friday from 10 a.m. till 3:30 p.m.

From the North

Take the Sallings River Drive to Grant Ave. (Exit 42). Follow the signs to Lincoln Avenue. Head south on Lincoln for 2 miles. Turn left on Grant Ave. The Gallery is located right on the corner.

From the South

Take the Elizabeth Parkway to Exit 42. At the traffic light, turn left onto Lincoln Avenue. Head south on Lincoln for 2 miles. Turn left on Grant Ave. The Gallery is located right on the corner.

Parking

Please park in our free parking lot. Metered street parking is available, but not recommended. There is a one-hour time limit on all meters. Additional parking is available in the area. Most lots charge of fee of $5 and up.

Special

June is Art Appreciation month in the area. Visitors can receive half off by using selected parking lots in the area if you are a member of an art appreciation society, such as the Anne Billings Society. Please speak to any one of our representatives here in the Gallery about becoming a member of the Anne Billings Art Appreciation Society.

Our Art Appreciation Society

Membership in the Society has many privileges. You will be placed on our preferred mailing list so you will know weeks in advance of any special upcoming events. As a member, you also are invited to participate in our monthly "Meet the Artist" socials. You can meet and greet some of the finest artists in the region, the nation, and the world. Don't delay in becoming a member of the Anne Billings Art Appreciation Society. As a member, you will also receive our free newsletter that includes artist profiles, a schedule of upcoming events, and discounts on admission, local parking, and great dining deals at local restaurants. We invite you to join today.
