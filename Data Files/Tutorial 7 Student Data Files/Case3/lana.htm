We've Got Room to Spare!

Come visit us for the grand opening of the Strike 'n Spare, our newest eatery here at Lana Lanes. We've served up a 50's style menu for you, complete with the best burgers and fries this side of the Mississippi. We've got malts and shakes in every flavor, as long as you ask for chocolate, vanilla, and strawberry, that is. All of our burgers are made from Texas prime beef, and our burgers are at least 90% lean. We cook our burgers well done over a flaming grill to give you that great seared taste of the Great Plains best burger. If you choose to skip the burger, then try our salad bar on for size. We've got a soup, salad, and breadstick combo platter that's sure to please.

Our summer spectacular continues with great music every Friday and Saturday night. Come dance in the Tailfin Lounge, where each week you can dance to some of the best sounds of the 50's. You've got your chance to sing karaoke on Wednesday nights, should you be in the mood to sing some of the best hits of the 50's. All of our seats are trimmed with chrome and real vinyl, so you'll be in the mood to stroll down memory lane with us any night of the week.

On Thursday nights, Doo wop meets hip hop. We turn the Tailfin Lounge into the place to dance to the hits of today. DJ "Ruth E" will have you dancing all night long to the sounds of this century. There is never a cover charge for admission to the Tailfin Lounge.

Summer Leagues

Summer Leagues are forming now. We have leagues for men, women, teens, seniors, singles, couples, and mixed teams. We also have a Little Tykes league that bowls on Saturday mornings. Parents must accompany children while games are in progress.

Family Fun Nights

Sunday nights are Family Fun Nights. We have discounted rates for families and for equipment rental. Enter our nightly raffle and win big prizes. Anyone bowling a perfect game receives free pizza for the entire family.

Specials

During the summer we have $2.00 games and $2.00 shoe rentals on Monday nights from 6 p.m. till 11 p.m. On Tuesday nights, we have $2.50 games and $1.00 shoe rentals.

E-Mail Club

Join our E-Mail club. You get special alerts on all our special deals, plus you get coupons available only to our online subscribers. The special offers are only available to those folks on our e-mail list; coupons not transferrable.

Fundraisers

Speak to one of our salespersons to learn about using Lana Lanes for you next fundraiser. There's lots to do here even if you don't bowl. We're indoors, so your event never gets rained out. Temperature is never a problem in our climate-controlled building. Your fundraisers accomplish two things — they raise money, and everyone gets to have a great time while they are here.

We can help you organize your next fund raiser right here. We can arrange for catering to meet any menu choices you want. Customized packages are never a problem for us. We can add or substitute dishes and beverages as requested. Changes in the menu may result in a small increase in the per person charge.

Birthday Parties

Our party package includes all of the following:

  • Each child receives one hour of free bowling.
  • Each child's choice of either hamburger, hot dog, slice of pizza, and fruit juice. Burgers and hot dogs are served with junior portion of fries.
  • Use of our private party room. Each party is hosted by one of our staff; we do all the work for your party while you sit back and enjoy your kids and their friends having fun.
  • One hour's use of the game room.
  • Sheet cake, plates, forks, and spoons.
  • Balloons and party decorations.
